Expanding on the Values

1. Inclusivity

At CPC, we embrace diversity and inclusivity. We believe that a strong community is built on the unique contributions of individuals from all backgrounds. Whether you’re an automotive expert or just starting to explore the world of motorsports, you’re welcome here. We strive to create a supportive and welcoming environment where everyone can share their passion and knowledge.

2. Passion for Motorsports

Our core is a shared passion for motorsports and automotive innovation. We’re united by the thrill of the race, the excitement of automotive performance, and the joy of working on and with vehicles. CPC encourages members to stoke their enthusiasm and grow their expertise, fueling our collective love for all things automotive.

3. Safety and Responsibility

Responsibility is paramount at CPC. We emphasize safe driving practices, responsible event organization, and a respect for the environment. We’re committed to ensuring that all members prioritize safety and conduct themselves in a responsible manner. Reckless driving, dangerous behavior, and disregard for safety have no place within our club.

4. Community Impact

We believe in giving back and making a positive impact on the community around us. Whether it’s through charity events, educational initiatives, or environmentally responsible practices, CPC is dedicated to contributing to the well-being of the communities we engage with. We aim to be a force for good, leaving a positive mark on the world.

5. Transparency and Accountability

Communication, transparency, and financial accountability are key values for CPC. We keep our members informed, involve them in decision-making processes, and ensure that our finances are managed with transparency and responsibility. As an organization, we prioritize open and honest dialogue, ensuring that all members have a voice in shaping our future and that our actions align with our values.
